This is Filipino soup Dish. Sinigang is a tamarind-based soup that is known for its sour and savory taste

Ingredients

  • Beef Short Ribs
  • Water
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Kang kong
  • Gabi
  • Radish
  • Tamarin Powder
  • finger chilis
  • Fish sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Procedure

  1. Simmer pork with water with onion, salt, pepper and tomatoes for 45 minutes.
  2. Add finger chilis, radish, and gabi with 2-min intervals
  3. Pour tamarin powder. Add fish sauce to taste. Stir in kangkong. Transfer into a serving bowl and serve immediately.
